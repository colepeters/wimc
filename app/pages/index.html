The Winnipeg Insight Meditation Community (WIMC) is a peer led, non sectarian sangha (or community) of meditation practitioners. We share a commitment to the study & practice of the dharma — the teachings of Buddhism — as a means to support one another in bringing awareness, compassion, and wisdom into our daily lives and the lives of our wider communities.

We meet each week on Sunday mornings for meditation and group discussion. All are welcome to join. To learn more, read about our weekly sessions. We recommend that those new to our sangha read our orientation before attending their first session.

As a peer led sangha, our areas of study and discussion change each week. To stay up to date with our sitting times, locations, upcoming discussion topics, and any special events, subscribe to our newsletter.
